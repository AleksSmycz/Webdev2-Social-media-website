YouTube

Overview

YouTube is a social media platform based on primarily on uploading videos to communicate with massive audiences, I is the second biggest search engine after google and is owned by Google. YouTube is amiable to anyone with an internet connection and is completely free. YouTube has a vast amount of video on any topic. It has the latest music and has a more comprehensive music library than most music apps. It has educational videos on history, geography, finance, computer science, and many other subjects which are completely free, YouTube also boasts many free tutorials on a wide range of topics being how to use Microsoft word or fixing a pipe leak.
