Instagram

Postives

  • Instagram is free for anyone with an internet connection.
  • It’s easily accessible to anyone from anywhere.
  • It’s easy to send out daily updates on Instagram.
  • It’s a good addition to the classroom.
  • It provides a good platform for photography courses.
  • It has a private messaging system.
  • It’s easy to share pictures and can do it in a couple of seconds.

Negatives

  • You need to make an account to view anything Instagram.
  • It’s easy to make fake accounts and troll content.
  • It doesn’t make sense to be used by itself.
  • It doesn’t make sense to use its full extent in some subjects.
  • It video feature is weak.
  • It’s easy to get distracted on Instagram.
