Whatsapp

Overview

WhatsApp is a free toll used to send messages through the internet it can be downloaded on desktop android phone and IPhone. It connects you with other phone users you have in your contacts and is cheaper than texting. It also has enhanced privacy features so your data and private messaging wont leak. It owned by Facebook .

Uses for learning

WhatsApp is primarily a messaging service video calls can be made through it but this feature is not available through the desktop. As of right now only 4 people are allowed to be in a video call at once however this is said to change in the future.
