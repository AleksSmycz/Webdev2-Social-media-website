Discord

Overview

Discord is a group chatting app originally made for gamers to communicate while playing video games. Discord is primarily a voice and video calling app with messaging capabilities similar to WhatsApp or messenger. It has the ability to host server that are easy to make and can host 100 or even thousands of people. These servers can be customised too allow to do many things e.g play music, roll dice, etc.

Uses for learning
