Messenger

Postives

  • It’s free and easy to access.
  • It’s available on most devices.
  • It has a large voice call feature.

Negatives

  • Its service can be choppy and messages might not send.
  • The quality of voice chat isn’t always the best.
  • You can only send 25mb files through messenger.
  • It’s less encrypted than WhatsApp.
  • It has adds inside the messaging app.
  • It’s not a great service by itself.
